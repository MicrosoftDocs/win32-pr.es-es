-   [Eventos de objeto RecognizerContext](recognizercontext-object-events.md)
-   [Eventos de la colección Strokes](strokes-collection-events.md)
-   [Eventos de control de InkEdit](inkedit-control-events.md)
-   [Eventos del control InkPicture](inkpicture-control-events.md)
-   [Eventos de objeto PenInputPanel](peninputpanel-object-events.md)
