<span data-ttu-id="f02a3-105">Direct3D 10, Direct3D 11 y Direct3D 12 usan DXGI.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f02a3-105">DXGI is used by Direct3D 10, Direct3D 11 and Direct3D 12.</span></span>
