| [**Graphics(Image \* )**](/windows/win32/api/gdiplusgraphics/nf-gdiplusgraphics-graphics-graphics(inimage))          | Crea un [**objeto Graphics::Graphics**](/windows/win32/api/gdiplusgraphics/nf-gdiplusgraphics-graphics-graphics(inimage)) asociado a un [**objeto Image.**](/windows/win32/api/gdiplusheaders/nl-gdiplusheaders-image)<br/>   |

| [**Gráficos (HDC, HANDLE)**](/windows/win32/api/gdiplusgraphics/nf-gdiplusgraphics-graphics-graphics(inhdc_inhandle)) | Crea un [**objeto Graphics::Graphics**](/windows/win32/api/gdiplusgraphics/nf-gdiplusgraphics-graphics-graphics(inhdc_inhandle)) asociado a un contexto de dispositivo especificado y a un dispositivo especificado.<br/> |
